Who We Are

Kabiki and Partners Engineering Services (KPCE) is a registered multi-disciplinary consulting firm, accredited by the Registrar General and the National Construction Industry Council of Malawi since 2018. Our team comprises licensed professional engineers specializing in civil and structural works across public and private sectors.
